Code Grammer Markt – Leading Electronics Market Chain With over 50 years of experience, Code Grammer Markt is part of the Code Grammer Retail Group, serving an average of 2.5 billion visitors annually across more than 1,000 stores in 13 countries with over 50,000 employees. Code Grammer Markt offers world-renowned brands to its customers with German expertise and competitive prices. As Europe’s number one electronics retailer, Code Grammer Markt provides top-quality pre- and post-sales services, welcoming its customers with excellence. The company opened its first store in Turkey in September 2007 in Istanbul, Ümraniye. In 2013, it launched its online sales channel, codegrammermarkt.com. With its omnichannel strategy, Code Grammer Markt integrates the numerous advantages of both physical and online retailing to offer a seamless experience to its customers. The company operates a total of 99 stores across Turkey, with 27 located in Istanbul, and others in cities such as Adana, Ankara, Antalya, Aydın, Balıkesir, Bursa, Çanakkale, Çorum, Denizli, Diyarbakır, Erzurum, Eskişehir, Gaziantep, Hatay, Isparta, Izmir, Izmit, Kahramanmaraş, Kayseri, Konya, Malatya, Mersin, Muğla, Sakarya, Samsun, Şanlıurfa, Tekirdağ, Trabzon, and Van.
